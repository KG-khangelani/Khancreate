Students who once learnt maths in high school are curious to understand how does maths relate or apply in the real world. As in science fiction it is always depicted as a language of the aliens or gods though it is the science of man.

The attempt with Expodigy is to unpack a mathematical function(equation) as example and explore how it may apply in multiple sociological contexts. As mathematics is the core complication of artificial intelligence.

The project will further explore and explain the properties of the function’s split elements. To explore the influence of each element on the graph and how it correlates to a sociological claim.
Students who do not study maths but curious of its potential implications.
Attempt to unpack a mathematical function (equation) and explore how it may apply to real world situations in a more anthropocentric context that is more relatable to the user. This experiment will take the form of an interactive website.
The goal is to use the formula to learn/predict something about the user. This engaging experience is to serve as practical example of how Ai works.
Research and investigate the philosophical and factual elements of Sci-Fi, Investigate its categories and its history and  then create an engaging experience for your target audience. Work to develop ideas that are not obvious and shine a new light on how Science/Fiction intertwine with our lives and give narratives to our experiences, both culturally, philosophically and theoretically.
