The goal is that the boy gets to
spend quality time with the mother
of his child to provide love the
necessary support to her and be
the couple goals he always see on
the social media. He takes all the
necessary risks to see her, be it
fighting her father and navigating
the dangerous township streets on
his own.
The problem is that the boy is still
teenager. As by culture he has not
compensated for the damage he
hath caused to his girlfriend and
her family as they are not married
yet. For that reason he is not
welcomed to be near the girl and
the expected child until the boy’s
family compensate for the
necessary damage. The father is
mad at what this boy has done to
his beautiful daughter as he wants
the boy nowhere near her.
A teenage boy learns that his
girlfriend is pregnant. He’s in
distress in receipt of these
shocking news. In remembrance of
the way he’s being raised up by a
single parent mother. He has
sworn to never be absent for his
children, for he inspires to be a
great father to them. Now that
things have happened faster than
expected. He’s in need of answers
of how he happened to make a
make a baby. While on the other
hand he wants to be a present
loving father to his pregnant
girlfriend and be supportive to
them.

For the boy to visit his baby and baby mama.
Prior to his unwelcomed visits he needs to
collect essentials for the baby, nappies and milk
bottles. Collect & save money to compensate for
the necessary needs for her and the damage he
has done to the family. He needs to navigate
himself around unsafe streets  eKasi (township)
with skollies in every corner.
With the number of teenage pregnancy escalating by an alarming number in South Africa.
The game is targeted at teenagers aged 16 - 18. More focused on the less represented
because of the infastructure accessibilty, the black community living in the township. This
game aimed at rasing awareness on the hardships & difficulties of teenage pregnancy as it is
not an ideal position to be in rather enjoy one’s youth and play games.
