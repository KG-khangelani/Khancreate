This project came from the issue that majority (21%) of South African residential fires in the informal settlements are cause by stoves and heaters.
Some of the first renditions of the User Interface chosen from for further developing.  The goal is that the interface be of a familiar aesthetic to the user, less intimidating and clear.  A play with primary shapes as foundation for building familiar structures.

The rectangular shape is ideal because it what most users are familiar with as it makes up most of the reliable structures and interfaces like the walls of our homes, the TV, the phone etc. Conveniently it is in harmony with the product's body design.
There's been a 13.5% annual increase in the number of informal settlements that are affected by fires in the townships. This no good news for low-income families who lose their belongings to these fires mainly caused by negligence and malfunction.

The aim was to design a mobile air-conditioner with a digital surface, combined with a stove to combat the dreadful conditions of living in an informal shelter, making more comfortable. With the design making use of Induction heating for cooking and air-multiplying technology for air-conditioning.
